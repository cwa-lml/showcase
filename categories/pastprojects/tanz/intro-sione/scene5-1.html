Leisure Time

Every Sunday my family goes to church. We speak Samoan at church. And my wife and I run the Sunday school there. I'm the coach of our local rugby team so that keeps me really busy. Mostly my weekends are around these kinds of things. I also enjoy music and I like to sing and play the guitar. Tofa soifua.
