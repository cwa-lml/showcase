About PDF files

Adobe Acrobat PDF format ensures your print-out closely matches the original paper version.

Your computer must have the Adobe Acrobat Reader application (version 3 or 4) installed to read a PDF document. If you do not have Adobe Acrobat Reader installed, you can download it free from www.adobe.com, by clicking on the 'Get Acrobat Reader' button.
