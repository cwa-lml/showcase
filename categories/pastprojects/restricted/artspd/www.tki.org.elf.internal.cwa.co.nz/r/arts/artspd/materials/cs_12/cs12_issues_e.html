Issues

Adequate time

Andy acknowledged that it took a long time to finish the paintings (20 hours) even though the students worked with concentration as they revisited their paintings over several lessons to modify and improve them. Without an adequate allotment of time, the finished work in both schools would have been less successful.

Protocols for using harakeke/flax

Māori protocols should be observed when using harakeke/flax (Phormium tenax, cultivated for weaving and plaiting). For instance, the need for conservation means that one cuts only the outside leaves and places all off-cuts back beneath the flax bush.
