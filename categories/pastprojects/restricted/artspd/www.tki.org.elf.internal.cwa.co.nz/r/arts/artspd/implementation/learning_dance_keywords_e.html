Dance Objectives - Key Words

by Polly Thin-Rabb, Dance Facilitator, University of Waikato

This reference provides teachers with a memory aid for planning dance units. It lays out the key action words (verbs) from the achievement objectives of the dance strands (in The Arts in the New Zealand Curriculum) alongside examples of key words that can be used in learning objectives related to these achievement objectives.
