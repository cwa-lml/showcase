Tane and the Stars – A Tane me ngā Whetu

A music unit plan for years 4–8

by Catherine Short, Music Adviser, University of Waikato

This unit explores the creating and playing of aspects of music. The lesson sequence contains four teaching sessions, which include activities related to sound compositions that are inspired by the story Tane and the Stars – A Tane me ngā Whetu by R. Bacon and M. Smith.
