  • Gather a wide stylistic collection of examples of mood music/programme music/filmmusic that particularly uses the devices of ostinato and textural change.
  • Encourage students to find own examples for discussion and own visual sources for motivation
  • Provide enough instruments and music technology equipment for group work.
  • Ensure each group has an able transcriber/software notation user to assist in the notation of music created. Music may simply be sequenced, notated and edited.
  • Read and use a range of texts to support your teaching programme (go to Teaching and Learning Materials)
