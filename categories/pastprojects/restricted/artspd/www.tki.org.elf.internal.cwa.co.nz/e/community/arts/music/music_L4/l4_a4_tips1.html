As a teacher:

  • gather a wide range of music that offers the opportunity to hear clear contrasts both within and between elements and between sections within the piece

  • provide ongoing opportunities for students to sketch/chart in a range of ways, the contrasts heard in pieces or in dance/music performances viewed

  • consider carefully how groups are formed

  • seek out enough different physical spaces within which groups can work

  • provide portable tape machines with built-in microphones for easy recording of sound-making and encourage students to help each other with recording process

  • establish with students and colleagues accepted safe practices (personal, physical space, use of and care for instruments)
