Soundscapes

Soundscapes are useful for evoking a mood and if possible each student should have the opportunity to experience it individually. An audiotape can provide this but a "live" way is to create a soundscape tunnel. Have all those contributing to the sound line up on two sides of the room about a metre apart. Have each person repeat their contributions until they are at the end of the tunnel and then it is their turn to walk through the sound. If there are enough people, being led by the hand blindfolded further enhances the experience.
