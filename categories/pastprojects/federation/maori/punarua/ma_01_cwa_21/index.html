He tau punarua

Arā ētahi tau nui. Mēnā ka wāwāhia ngā tau nui ki ētahi tau iti e rua (arā, he tau punarua), he māmā ake te whakaoti tāpiritanga i roto i tō hinengaro. He nui ngā tau punarua mō te tau kotahi. Hei tauira, ko te 7 me te 8 tētahi o ngā tau punarua mō te 15. Tāpirihia te 7 me te 8, ka 15.


Ko te 11 me te 4 tētahi anō tau punarua mō te 15. Tāpirihia te 11 me te 4, ka 15.

Whakaarohia ētahi tau punarua anō mō te 15.

Ko te 6 me te 9 tētahi.

He ōrite te tau punarua 9 me te 6, ko te raupapa noa iho o ngā tau te mea e rerekē ana.

Ko te 10 me te 5 tētahi tau punarua anō mō te 15.

He ōrite te 5 me te 10, ko te raupapa noa iho o ngā tau te mea e rerekē ana.

He aha ētahi atu tau punarua mō te 15?

Tuhia ngā tau punarua katoa mō te 15. (E 7 katoa ngā tau punarua mō te 15).
