Hangaia āu ake whakareatanga

  1. Tuhia ngā tau o tō whakareatanga ki konei.
  2. Pāwhiria te pūtohu Whakawātea, hei whakangaro atu i ngā tau.
  3. Pāwhiria te pūtohu Whiriwhiria kia puta ai te Papa Whakarea.
