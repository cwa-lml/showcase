Te whakaawhiwhi ki te tekau tata

Ko tā tēnei rautaki, he kawe i tētahi o ngā tau ki runga, ki te tekau e pātata ana. He māmā te whakarea ki te tekau, engari kaua e wareware ki te tango atu i ngā tau i tāpirihia.

Hei tauira, ko te 38 x 4. Whakaawhiwhia te 38 ki te 40. Whakareatia te 40 ki te 4, ka 160. I tāpirihia te 2 ki te 38 kia eke ki te 40, nō reira e 2 ngā 4 te rahinga kē atu o te whakareatanga. Nō reira ka tangohia atu te 8 kia tae atu ki te otinga whakamutunga, te 152.

Whakamahia tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i te 9 x 17.



I eke anō tō otinga ki te 153?

Whakareatia te 17 ki te 10, ka 170. Kotahi te 17 te rahinga kē atu o tēnei whakareatanga, nō reira me tango atu te 17 i te 170, ko te 153 te otinga whakamutunga.

E taea ana tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i tētahi whakareatanga uaua ake, perā i te 12 x 38.

Ka whakaawhiwhi au i te 38 ki te 40, nā te mea e mōhio ana au ki te whakareatanga 12 x 40, arā, ka 480. Kātahi ka tangohia atu e 2 ngā 12, arā, te 24.

Tangohia te 24 i te 480, ka 456. Whakamahia tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i te 28 x 17.

Ko te 476 tō otinga whakamutunga?

Whakareatia te 30 ki te 17, ka eke ki te 510. Tangohia e 2 ngā 17 (arā, te 34), ko te 476 te otinga whakamutunga.
