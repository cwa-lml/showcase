    WAC: the long tail

    • The long tail: the consumption of content "after the rush"
    • Analytics show long tail audience is more focused
      - time on site is much higher
    • For good content, long tail consumption will out-weigh head
    •  Analysis of content that works in the long tail shows two spikes:
      - Reading age: 6 years education
      - Reading age: 13 years education
    • Used Flesch-Kincaid to analyse content

    WAC: Community Engagement

    • WAC thinking laterally about community engagement
    • Have an adjoining open field, used for many events, including annula rock concert
    • Decided to focus on what the community wanted, not what the WAC wanted
    • Answer: internet cat video festival!
      - massively popular locally
      - lots of national coverage
      - 3% of all site visitors from that point on due to cat video festival
