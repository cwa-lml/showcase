School Days

When I went to school here I thought the teachers didn't really know about my language or my culture, and so they couldn't really give me the help I needed. I had one teacher who used to make me read out loud in front of the class and I felt ashamed because I wasn't very good at that, so I used to skip school and go to the shops and play spacies instead.
