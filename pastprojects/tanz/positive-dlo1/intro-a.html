Introduction

The scenario

The CEO isn’t happy. A report was completed two weeks ago but the statistics section was badly written, some vital information was missing, and other information was incorrect. The proverbial has hit the fan.

Charlotte and her team are fielding the complaint.

Your task

View the slide show, consider how the characters relate to each other and then carefully consider the KEY QUESTIONS at the end of each scene.

Once you have reviewed the Summary, reflect on the issues the scenario raises and how they relate to your own work experience. Write up your considerations in your Reflective Journal (download the template from your Toolkit in Blackboard).
