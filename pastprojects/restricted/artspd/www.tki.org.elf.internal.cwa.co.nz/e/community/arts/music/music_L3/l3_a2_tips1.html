Teaching Tips

As a teacher, take the time to:

  • gather a range of sound sources

  • experiment with contrast in music

  • model contrast with sound

  • provide ongoing opportunities for students to explore sound sources individually and in small groups

  • seek out different physical spaces for sound exploration to occur

  • establish with students and colleagues accepted safe practices (personal, physical space, use of and care for instruments)

  • provide a range of student grouping opportunities
