Focus

Props can help with the focus – a large square of coloured cloth can become many things, as can several sturdy black boxes (strong enough to stand on, small enough to move easily). A dress-up box and a collection of simple instruments can all assist with focus. Physically move students into the places and shapes that you want them in. Tell them that they have to remember where they are and what shape they are in and may have to hold that position for some time (no balancing on one foot).
