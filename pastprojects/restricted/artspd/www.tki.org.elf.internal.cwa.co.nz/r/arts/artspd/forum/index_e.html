Archived Forum Discussion

Below are excerpts from the weekly forum discussions which are held on Wednesday afternoons from 4 – 5pm. Each discussion focuses on one of the four arts disciplines – dance, drama, music, or the visual arts. An arts "expert", such as a National Coordinator – The Arts, or an arts celebrity such as Gareth Farr, is an invited guest online to respond to teachers' questions.
