Te Tekau me te Tahi

Whakamahia tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i te 24 + 13.

Tuatahi, tāpirihia te 20 me te 10.

Kātahi ka tāpiri i te 4 me te 3.

Ko te mea whakamutunga, ka tāpiri i ngā otinga e rua nei.

I eke anō te otinga ki te 37?

Whakamahia tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i te 33 + 62 i roto i tō hinengaro.

Ko tēnei rautaki, he tāpiri i ngā tekau, he tāpiri anō hoki i ngā tahi. Kātahi ka tāpiri i ngā otinga e rua.

Ko te 24 + 28 hei tauira. Ka wāwāhia ngā tekau me ngā tahi, arā, he rite ki te 20 + 4, me te 20 + 8.

Ka tāpirihia te 20 me te 20, ka 40. Ka tāpirihia te 4 me te 8, ka 12.

Kātahi ka tāpiri i te 40 me te 12, ko te 52 te otinga.
