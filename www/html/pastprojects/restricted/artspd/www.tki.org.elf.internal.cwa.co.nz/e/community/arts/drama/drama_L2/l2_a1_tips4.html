Voice

  • Spend some time with the students experimenting with using their voice.
  • Loud and soft, high and low, a voiced whisper and talking softly.
  • Simple tongue twisters can be fun:
      Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers.
      She sells seashells on the seashore.
      What noise annoys an oyster most, a noisy noise annoys an oyster most.

  • Then you can ask questions and get the students to answer using only inflection:
      What does she do on the seashore?
      She sells seashells on the seashore.
      What does she sell?
      She sells seashells on the seashore.
      Where does she sell?
      She sells seashells on the seashore.
