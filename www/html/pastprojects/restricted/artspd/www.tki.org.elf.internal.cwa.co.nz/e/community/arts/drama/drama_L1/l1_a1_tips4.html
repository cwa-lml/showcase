Tension

Once the tension is introduced consider following it through and start to create a story – someone is late, what are they going to do? How do they feel? (The late person need never actually arrive). It is through the tension that the drama is created. The students can express their feelings by freezing the whole group and the teacher going round several (or all) of the students, touching them on the shoulder and asking them to tell the whole group how they feel. [Note: feelings are powerful and they are what makes drama a powerful medium – they can also trigger other feelings in the students from earlier memories. If this happens it needs to be handled sensitively.]
