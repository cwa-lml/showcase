Focus

Talk to the students about this before the drama begins.
Have a discussion on what draws our attention and what dissipates our attention.
Eyes – where do they look?, open or half closed?
Mouths – open, relaxed or tight, smiling or grim?
Hands – open palm or clenched fists, limp or rigid?
Gestures
Proximity/distance
Relative height
Touch
An object in the scene
Make sure there is a singular focus unless a sense of confusion or dissipation is what is required.
Focus as outlined in this lesson would make an ideal reflection at the conclusion of the work.
