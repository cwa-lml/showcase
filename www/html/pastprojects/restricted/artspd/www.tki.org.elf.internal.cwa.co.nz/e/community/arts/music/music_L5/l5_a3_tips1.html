  • Gather a wide stylistic collection of suitable group performance music
  • Encourage students to find own examples of versions of music they are listening to or practising for playbacks and warmups.
  • Provide enough tuned instruments for class work. If available, encourage use of computer music programs like Band in a Box. to assist playalong.
  • Provide video cameras and encourage students to record and evaluate their progress frequently.
  • Read and use a range of texts to support your teaching programme such as Busker's books, Fake It books, Kaleidoscope series arrangements and so on (go to Teaching and Learning Materials)
  • Check out performance venues and acoustics to ensure the best outcome.
