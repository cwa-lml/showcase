Props

Props are invaluable and can be simple but should always be authentic – students will be disappointed if the photo is not in the letter or the card is made out to the wrong name. A props basket, a range of simple costumes especially hats/wigs/glasses, a box of simple instruments and squares of many different coloured and textured material can be combined and recombined to make endless variations. Some of the props may even take on a symbolic significance for the class – a particular hat may always denote superiority, a piece of cloth may have magic properties.
