As a teacher, take the time to:

  • Gather and explore extracts of different media music
  • Provide ongoing opportunities for students to hear a variety of music for different purposes
  • Model brief examples of improvised or composed music (ie teacher or student to share a brief basic example of a reworked existing song/piece for a new situation)
  • Investigate resources such as Film Music for use in class.
