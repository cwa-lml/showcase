Teacher Issues

How we meet the requirements of all four disciplines

Question

How can teachers meet requirements when the arts are structured as four distinct disciplines (dance, drama, music, visual arts) and when developing skills, knowledge, attitudes, and understanding in one discipline does not imply a similar development in another?

Background

Requirements
  • In years 1 to 8, students must study and have the opportunity to meet achievement objectives in all four disciplines.
  • Programmes in the arts disciplines will provide learning opportunities in each of the four strands.

Unique/different

  • Each discipline has its own distinctive body of knowledge.
  • Students are expected to develop informed attitudes towards traditional and contemporary modes of expression in each discipline.
  • Literacy in the art forms and genres of one culture or period does not imply literacy in those of another culture or period.

Integrating strands

The overlapping nature of the strands encourages integrated learning experiences. This means that, in a given project or unit of work, students will generally learn in more than one strand at the same time for each discipline.

Answer

Teachers can meet the requirements by using a range of strategies. These will require careful planning and could include:

  • combining learning in two or more arts disciplines for example, waiata-a-ringa in which both music and dance are integral parts; by using visual arts as essential components of production in dance, drama or music;
  • linking the arts disciplines to other essential learning areas;
  • developing multimedia or multidisciplinary projects;
  • through school-wide events such as festivals, productions, or cultural celebrations;
  • using flexibility in determining weightings given to individual disciplines to the meet the needs of students.

Key considerations
Assessment
teachers need to:
  • identify the different forms of learning that occur
  • monitor students progress and achievement in each arts discipline

Arts and integration across essential learning areas
teachers need to:

  • ensure that learning experiences make appropriate and meaningful links between the arts and other learning areas
