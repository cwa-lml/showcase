How to Start

The professional development process that you are undertaking will follow a pathway of review, planning, implementation, and review. It is important that you proceed at your own pace and select material appropriate to your school's needs. Web links are provided to assist you through the process. We suggest that you print this page and use it as a reference.

>  Review  >  Plan  >  Implement  >  Evaluate  >  Review  >

Here is an outline of one possible pathway through the site.


Review

  • Begin by becoming familiar with The Arts in the New Zealand Curriculum document available online at http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/curriculum/statement/contents_e.php.


  • As part of your professional development, go to Guide to the Document, getting to know the new curriculum, http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/materials/ohts2_e.php.


  • We suggest you first complete activities 1. Orientation, and 2. Brainstorm "What do we mean by The Arts?". From the list of activities 3. to 17., select discussion starters that will be appropriate to your professional development needs.


To help identify your needs, those enrolled in The Arts Online Professional Development contract will receive an ICT Accessibility form and a Contact Person Review form. Answer the questions on these forms and send a copy to Multi Serve, PO Box 19059, Wellington.

The next step could be to:

  • use the School Self Review form available online as a basis for discussion. Select http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/materials/reviewforms_e.php.

Teachers participating in The Arts Online contract should send a copy of their review form and development plan to CLangton@multiserve.co.nz for professional comment.


Plan

When planning your programme, ask questions such as:

  • Who will be involved? How will that be arranged? What will you do? What resources are required? What is your timeline?


  • Choose "Professional Development Models" to help you plan and manage your programme in school: http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/materials/pdmodels1_e.php


  • For planning assistance select "School Development Plan": http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/implementation/plans2_e.php


  • Choose "Sample Arts Policy" for an example: http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/implementation/policy3_e.php

Implement

  • For a description of a curriculum delivery plan and access to planning templates for all four disciplines, select "Curriculum Plans": http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/implementation/plans_e.php


  • To read about how other schools have approached implementation of the new curriculum document select "Case Studies": http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/materials/cs_e.php


  • For a summary of the key points of assessment and reporting procedures, select "Assessment and Reporting": http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/artspd/implementation/assess_e.php


  • To view a range of trial exemplars select The National Exemplars Trial: http://www.tki.org.nz/r/assessment/exemplars/index_e.php



Evaluate and Review

To evaluate your programme, conduct another review. Re-visit your school's development plan, assess how it has achieved its goals, and determine what on-going arts professional development your school needs.
