Elements of dance

This reference material provides teachers with a memory aid for planning and teaching dance units. It lays out the elements of dance referred to in The Arts in the New Zealand Curriculum under useful trigger questions alongside the descriptive words associated with each of the elements on the Dance Wall Charts (which were supplied free to all New Zealand schools by the Ministry of Education).

Source: Page 31 of the booklet from the Ministry of Education's multimedia resource Dancing the Long White Cloud: Teaching dance in years 1–10 (Wellington: Learning Media, 2002)
