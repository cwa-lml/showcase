Ngā tekau me ngā tahi

Ko tā tēnei rautaki, he wāwāhi i ngā tau ki ētahi tau he māmā noa iho te whakarea.

Hei tauira, ka whakareatia te 39 ki te 4.

Ka wāwāhia te 39 ki ōna tekau me ōna tahi, arā, te 30 me te 9.

Kātahi ka whakareatia te 30 ki te 4, ka whakareatia hoki te 9 ki te 4. Whakareatia te 30 ki te 4, ka 120.

Whakareatia te 9 ki te 4, ka 36.

Kātahi ka tāpirihia te 120 me te 36, ka eke ki te 156.

E taea ana tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i tētahi whakareatanga uaua ake, pērā i te 22 x 38. I konei, ka wāwāhia ngā tau e rua ki ngā tekau me ngā tahi.

Arā, he rite te 22 ki te 20 me te 2. He rite te 38 ki te 30 me te 8.

Ka wāwāhia te whakareatanga 22 x 38 ki ētahi whakareatanga māmā e 4:

Whakareatia te 20 ki te 30;

Whakareatia te 20 ki te 8;

Whakareatia te 2 ki te 30;

Whakareatia te 2 ki te 8. Ina tāpirihia ngā otinga katoa o ēnei whakareatanga, koirā te otinga o te 22 x 38. E taea ana i roto i tō hinengaro?

Whakareatia te 20 ki te 30, ka hia?

Whakareatia te 20 ki te 8, ka hia?

Whakareatia te 2 ki te 30, ka hia?

Whakareatia te 2 ki te 8, ka hia?
