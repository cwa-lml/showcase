Te Whakarearua

Ko tēnei rautaki, he whakamahi i ngā tau rearua hei whakaoti i te tāpiritanga.

Hei tauira, ka tāpirihia te 18 me te 8. Ka wāwāhia te 18 ki te 10 me te 8. Ināianei kua hurihia te tāpiritanga ki te 10 + 8 + 8. Ka whakarearua i te 8, ka 16. Ka tāpirihia te 10, ka 26.

Whakamahia tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i te 26 + 6.

Ka wāwāhia te 26 ki te 20 me te 6. Ināianei kua hurihia te tāpiritanga ki te 20 + 6 + 6.

Ka whakarearua i te 6, ka 12.

Ka tāpirihia te 20 me te 12.

I eke anō te otinga ki te 32?

Whakamahia tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i te 45 + 46 i roto i tō hinengaro.

He pai hoki tēnei rautaki hei whakaoti i ngā tāpiritanga uaua ake, pērā i te 24 + 28.

Tuatahi, ka tangohia te 4 i te 28, ka 24.

Tuarua ka whakarearuatia tia te 24, ka 48.

Kātahi ka tāpiri i te 4 i tangohia ake. Arā, tāpirihia te 4 ki te 48, ka 52.
