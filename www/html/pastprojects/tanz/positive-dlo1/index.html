Welcome to Positive Workplace Relationships

This interactive slide show will help you reflect on how you work with others in a team. It uses pictures, text and sound to tell the story of five employees who are dealing with a serious complaint.

Run through the interactive slide show a couple of times and reflect on the situation and the questions posed at the end of each scene. Make notes in your Reflective Journal. You will find this very useful later on.

Before you start a little technical information:

  • Select LOW if you are using a dial-up Internet connection. If you wish to hear the dialogue, check that your computer has speakers and can play audio files. Then select HIGH. Be patient. Sound files may take time to load.
