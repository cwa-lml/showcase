Sione the Baker

This interactive slide show will help you get to know Sione, one of the people you'll be working with in this module. It uses photos, text and audio to tell Sione's story and to give you important information about his background.

Run through the interactive slide show a few times and reflect on the things that Sione tells you about himself.

The Background section at the end of Sione's story briefly outlines some of the economic, social, cultural and political conditions that may have influenced his development, his attitudes, education and view of himself and his world. Make some notes about some of the things that may contribute to Sione's issues around literacy for use later in this and subsequent modules.

The final slide provides a full text transcription of Sione's biography in a PDF file that can be downloaded and printed.
