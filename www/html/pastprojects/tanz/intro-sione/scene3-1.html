Literacy Issues

I left school to start work, and my older sister, she used to help with my reading and if I needed to fill out forms or anything like that, and then I got married. My wife is a New Zealand Samoan, so she was born in New Zealand. She has a good job. She works as an office administrator. She helps me a lot with my reading and writing. We have five kids. I love them all very much. We want them to work hard at school and get a good education so my wife and I encourage them to read at home as much as we can and we have lots of books in our house. We speak mostly Samoan at home but English for the kids' homework.
