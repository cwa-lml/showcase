Welcome to Road User charges

This interactive slide show will help you get to know more about Sione, one of the people you'll be working with in this and later modules. It uses photos, text and audio to tell Sione's story and to give you important information about his literacy issues.

Run through the interactive slide show a few times and reflect on the things that Sione reveals about himself as he works through this task. Make notes about some of things that Sione seems to have trouble with in terms of his issues around literacy for use later in this and subsequent modules.

The final slide provides a summary of this scenario and a full text transcription in a PDF file that can be downloaded and printed.
