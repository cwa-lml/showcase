CWA Hot Shots

Hot Shots is sponsored by CWA New Media and provides young people with training in effective digital storytelling using video. Examples of their work follow:

  • A look at the emerging talent in Peter Jackson’s home town – creative city of Wellington www.hotshots.org.nz
  • A behind the scenes documentary about a major youth-focused UNESCO event held in Wellington www.livingfuture.org.nz
  • Young people tell the story of a young boy trying to make friends in a new school “Seasons” http://www.tki.org.nz/r/wick_ed/wickedtv/seasons.php
  • On location at the National Youth Arts festival http://www.tki.org.nz/r/arts/student_gallery/youth_arts_2003/index_e.php
  • On location with the climate change scientists at NIWA http://www.biotechlearn.org.nz/multimedia/hotshots_student_videos
  • Pasifika young people share their perspectives of growing up http://www.tki.org.nz/r/pasifika/hotshots/movies/index_e.php
